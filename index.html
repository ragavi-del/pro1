The Melvisharam Muslim Educational Society founded in 1918 that strove hard to metamorphose Primary, Secondary and Higher Education into splendid segments of pristine enlightenment irrespective of caste, creed, community or social status.

Welcome to M.M.E.S. Arts and Science College, an institution committed to academic excellence, ethical values, and holistic development. Our college provides quality education in arts, science, and commerce disciplines through a student-centered approach supported by experienced faculty and modern infrastructure. We focus on nurturing intellectual growth, critical thinking, and social responsibility, enabling students to achieve academic success and contribute meaningfully to society. With a supportive learning environment and emphasis on discipline and values, M.M.E.S. Arts and Science College strives to shape confident, skilled, and responsible graduates.

About Us

M.M.E.S Women’s Arts and Science College, established in 2007, is a Minority Institution affiliated with Thiruvalluvar University and ISO certified. The college offers 12 undergraduate, 7 postgraduate, and 2 research programmes, serving over 2050 students with a team of 92 qualified faculty members. M.M.E.S provides a vibrant learning environment with modern infrastructure, digital library facilities, remedial coaching, and value-added courses like NCVRT, DMLT, and Tally ERP 9. Its Career Guidance and Placement Cell actively supports students in securing placements. With active units such as NSS, YRC, and RRC, the college promotes holistic development through academics, research, and community engagement.
